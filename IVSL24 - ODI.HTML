Second ODI Highlights - August 4, 2024

In an exciting second ODI of the series, Sri Lanka emerged victorious over India by 32 runs. Batting first, Sri Lanka posted a total of 236 runs, setting a target of 237 for India. The innings started with an early setback as Pathum Nissanka was dismissed for a golden duck on the first ball by Mohammed Siraj. Despite no player scoring above 50, crucial contributions from the middle and lower order ensured a competitive score.

India's bowlers had a mixed day, with Mohammed Siraj taking one wicket. Kuldeep Yadav and Axar Patel claimed two wickets each, while Washington Sundar led the attack with three wickets. Despite their efforts, Sri Lanka's batsmen managed to keep the scoreboard ticking, setting a challenging target for India.

In response, India started positively with Rohit Sharma hitting his second consecutive fifty, scoring 64. However, India faced a middle-order batting collapse, which hampered their chase. Jeffrey Vandersay was the standout bowler for Sri Lanka, taking six wickets, while Charith Asalanka contributed significantly with three wickets. Except for Rohit Sharma, who scored 64, and Shubman Gill, who scored 34, no other Indian batsman managed to surpass 34. Despite a fighting effort, India was bowled out for 204, falling short by 32 runs.

This victory gave Sri Lanka a 1-0 lead in the series after the first ODI ended in a tie. With only one match remaining, Sri Lanka will look to secure the series win, while India will aim to level the series in the final match.
